BeerGears

A Twist on Your Favourite Drinking Game

BeerGears was created by me and my group during the McMaster Designathon. We paired with the Drunk Engineers to create a new and innovative drinking game

This game is a mechaism which constist of many gears with cups attached to the circumfrance. Once the game starts, the motor in the middle will move the gears.The objective is to sink balls in eachothers cups while the gears move.
