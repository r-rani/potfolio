Recycling Drone

Engineering 1P13 Project

As society pushes towards more eco-friendly methods of processing and disposing of goods many countries have been forced to revaluate their disposal systems. In this project, we created a vehicle for recylcing to travel in from a pick up station, to the correct bin

This project required my team to create a drone using autodesk inventor. We also had to be able to do a motion simulation of the drones movement.We used a linear actuator to turn the gears which lifted the hopper
